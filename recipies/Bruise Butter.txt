Bruise Butter
1 1/2 C calendula flowers (pot marigold)
8 oz jar pure lanolin (anhydrous)
6 oz coconut oil
a quarter to a half cup comfrey

Put all ingredients in a covered casserole, bake at 375 for an hour. Strain through cheesecloth into container, cool and label.

Many recommend adding some cloves or whatever to improve the smell. If it turns out too soft, melt it again and add some beeswax.
